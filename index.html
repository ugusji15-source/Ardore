Our Philosophy

Cooking with intention, serving with soul

Ardore — Italian for ardour, for heat, for longing — was born from a single obsession: the idea that the best meal you've ever had was probably a simple one, made with care. We source from small Italian producers, make every pasta by hand, and keep our menu short so each dish earns its place.

Chef Giulia Romani brings twelve years of experience from kitchens in Florence, Naples, and London. Her cooking never hides behind technique. It trusts the ingredient.
